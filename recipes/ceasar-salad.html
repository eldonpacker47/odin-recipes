Caesar Salad Recipe

Traditional Caesar salad. Makes 4 side-dish portions, or make it a main dish by adding some chicken or fish!

You may even have left over dressing! It keeps well in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Igredients

  • 1/3 cup mayo
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • salt, to taste
  • ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons fresh grated Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • shaved Parmigiano Reggiano (optional)
  • 1 cup Caesar salad croutons

Directions

Step 1

  1. Place mayo, lemon juice, garlic, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper in food processor. Process until smooth. Transfer to a small bowl and stir in grated cheese.

Step 2

  1. Place lettuce in a large salad bowl. Add a few dollops of dressing on the salad, and stir until all pieces are lightly coated with the dressing. Sprinkle croutons and top with shaved cheese.
