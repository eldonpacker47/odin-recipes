Baked Potato Soup Recipe

Baked potato soup is the perfect hearty, filling, stick-to-your-bones dinner for a

cold winter's night. This soup recipe will become a family favorite.

Baked Potatop Soup Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Butter
  • Flour and milk
  • Baked Potatoes
  • Seasoning
  • Cheese and sour cream

Best Potatoes for The Soup

Russet potatoes are perfect for this baked potato soup, as they'll break down

beautifully during the cooking process. You can use Yukon golds, but you might

have to cook the soup a bit longer.

Directions

  1. Cook the bacon.
  2. Melt the butter, then whisk the flour and milk.
  3. Add the potatoes and onions and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce to a simmer, then stir in remaining ingredients.
  5. Cook until the cheese is melted.
